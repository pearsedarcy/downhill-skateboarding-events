What is Downhill Skateboarding?

Downhill skateboarding is an extreme sport where riders navigate down steep hills and mountain roads at high speeds. Using specialized longboards equipped with precision trucks and wheels, riders can reach speeds exceeding 60 mph (97 km/h).

The sport combines technical skill, strategic thinking, and nerves of steel. Riders must master techniques like sliding, drafting, and cornering while maintaining control at high speeds.

Key Elements

  • High-speed racing and freeriding
  • Technical mountain roads
  • Safety gear and protection
  • Worldwide community
